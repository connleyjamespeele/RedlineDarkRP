Gambling module
================

This module provides the coin flip duel system.

Files:
- `sv_flips.lua`: server-side challenge creation, acceptance, and payout logic.
- `cl_flips.lua`: client-side menu to create a flip or accept someone else's challenge.

Commands:
- `/flips`: opens the flips menu.
- `/flip`: alias for `/flips`.
- `/acceptflip <name>`: accept another player's challenge from chat.
- `/flips cancel`: cancel your open challenge and get your money back.

Gameplay:
- One player opens a challenge with a bet amount and a call (heads/tails).
- Another player accepts the challenge.
- The winner takes the full pot.
